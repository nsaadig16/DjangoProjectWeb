Discover how to collect and trade cards in our TCG.

Card Collection

Cards are divided into different rarities and can be collected to build the perfect deck.

Image

Rarities

  • Common: Easy to find in packs.
  • Rare: Harder to obtain, with unique abilities.
  • Epic: Powerful and valuable cards.
  • Legendary: The most exclusive cards in the game.
Image

Card Trading

Players can trade cards with others to complete their collection.

  • Find players with cards you need.
  • Propose a fair trade based on rarity and value.
  • Expand your collection and improve your deck.
Image

Expansions and Updates

New expansions are regularly released with unique cards and innovative mechanics.

  • Stay tuned for new sets.
  • Discover special limited-edition cards.
  • Update your deck with the latest strategies.
